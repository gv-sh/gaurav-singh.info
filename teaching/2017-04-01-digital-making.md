The workshop aimed to introduce first-year design students to Object Oriented Design and how to apply it to develop new interactions and interfaces. Through the workshop, students learned key principles and concepts from this paradigm, which they also used to break down popular products to understand how they might have worked. They also gained experience in using Processing IDE + Processing Android to build tangible prototypes.

| Day       | Topics Covered                                      |
|-----------|-----------------------------------------------------|
| Day 1     | Introduction to OOD - Why Code? - Hello World - Simple Program |
| Day 2     | Thinking through OOD Artefacts Part 1 - Keywords - Literals - Grammar |
| Day 3     | Thinking through OOD Artefacts Part 2 - Datatypes - Functions |
| Day 4     | OOD Concepts Part 1 - Iterators - Classes           |
| Day 5     | OOD Concepts Part 2 - Activity 1 - Workshop Project Discussion |
| Day 6     | Intro to DP - Classes (cont.) - Presenting Ideas/Sketches |
| Day 7     | Intro to UML - Working on Project Day 1             |
| Day 8     | OOD Design Principles - Working on Project Day 2    |
| Day 9     | Final touches - Demo Presentation - Submit Reflection |
| Day 10    | Exhibition                                           |
