Introduction and Vision

Hello, I'm Gaurav Singh. At the heart of my work lies a simple yet profound goal: to blend technology and design in creating solutions that resonate deeply with human needs. My current endeavor at Griffith University focuses on the exciting intersection of machine learning, human-computer interaction, and engineering. My journey is a testament to the power of marrying artistic creativity with technological innovation, particularly in areas like data visualization, image processing, and ethical tech applications.

Educator. In my 7 years at Srishti-Manipal Institute, I've guided the future of design and technology by teaching 30 diverse courses. My role has been more than an instructor; I've been a mentor, a curriculum innovator, and an organizer of academic events that challenge and inspire students. One of my most fulfilling experiences was leading a project that transformed students' perspectives on computational creativity.

Technologist. I've used technology as a canvas for creativity, notably in the 'ReRide' project and my collaboration with Art in Transit. My approach uniquely integrates technology with education and industry, pushing the boundaries of how we use tech in creative contexts.
